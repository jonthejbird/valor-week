ValorWeek Rules:

1.) Free users are limited to 5 goals a week.
2.) Goals must be unique no repeats.
3.) Can set frequency of goals for a predefined point value.
4.) Weekly breakdown will have most productive day of the week and what goals have and haven't been fulfilled.
5.) A leaderboard will be displayed for free and premium members.
6.) You can have different profiles for different times in your life locked for free users.
7.) You can use others goals lists and save it for tracking this will alwasy start a new tracking session when activated.
